They might have different specialist area in the IT career, but they still share something in common. Their job mostly works with computer-related job, such as reviewing codes, maintaining the company’s websites, services, controlling and contributing data for the company’s database. Also, they might need to work with the hardware and know how to maintain them. The hardware is mostly from gadgets, or robots, or technology inventions. Mostly, the IT professionals works independently on their own when they are coding or doing their tasks, when it comes to some big project or create new thing, they discuss the idea and the way to contribute it with other IT professionals, their colleagues. They spend most of their time at the company or at home, somewhere with the computer or their personal laptop so they can get to work anytime, anywhere. Additionally, we all know that Silicon Valley is the place where many big technology companies such as Apple, Google, Android, Facebook, etc. put their head/flagship companies. Because of that, there are many IT professionals working in those companies that are located at Silicon Valley. There are many different challenges that the IT professionals need to face but the hardest, as my own understanding after I watched those videos, is they need to make their knowledge about information up to date. The world is changing and developing very fast so the information we read 2, 3 months ago can be expired very soon. Understanding the concepts of IT is hard but maintaining and updating the knowledge day by day is harder. If they do not update their knowledge, someday they will stay behind, and this problem might create the big skills gap between IT workers and many of them might be fired from their positions.
